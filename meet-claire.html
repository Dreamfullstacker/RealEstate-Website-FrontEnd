Meet Claire

CLAIRE ACKERMAN, REALTOR

THE ACKERMAN TEAM NORTH&CO

“Real success is finding your lifework in the work that you LOVE.” — David McCullough

Claire is a multi-degreed business professional that has been in the Real Estate industry since 2002. Her experience began in title & escrow in Mt. Airy Maryland.

Upon starting her career as a realtor in 2005 the Real Estate market took a turn for the worst. Claire listed & sold an abundance of distressed homes including, foreclosure & short sales. After experiencing the crash of the real estate market while witnessing another market shift all with a large decrease in inventory, she was presented with an opportunity to source properties to several hedge funds, which then created a niche of becoming an “Off Market Specialist”. Today this is mostly commonly known as an agent selling pocket listings. With strategic marketing & years of experience in the local real estate market, Claire is able to assist sellers in selling their homes as “pocket listings”, “coming soon” & providing a “first look” opportunity for her buyers. This alone provides a competitive edge for all of her clients alike.

Claire has always been passionate about assisting both buyer & sellers & concentrates on representing them with integrity, dedication, knowledge & is a powerhouse when negotiating the absolute best deals on their behalf. Claire is committed to educating & helping other professionals scale their business & provides a culture that attracts dedicated & passionate agents while striving to raise the bar in the Real Estate Industry. #passionpurposeperseverance #helpingonepersonatatime #ourclientsbecomeourfamily.

Let the team prove to you that they are “A Legacy of Different” & will deliver a real estate experience unlike any other! IT IS ABOUT YOU! Claire is Featured on of The American Dream TV which airs on CBS Channel 5. In which this showcases her listings and upcoming listing opportunities while highlighting the Community & Lifestyle that surround each property.

Recent Achievements include: Top 100 Realtors 2020 / Power Player on The American Dream on CBS / Featured in TOP AGENT Magazine / Top 1% of Realtors / Top 40 Realtors under 40 awarded 2015 – 2020 / Ranked Top 100 Most Influential Realtors in AZ / Featured in FORBES / 2019 Awarded Small Business Excellence Award in Scottsdale
