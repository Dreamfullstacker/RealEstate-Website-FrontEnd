01 Meet Claire Ackerman

Claire is a multi-degreed business professional that has been in the Real Estate industry since 2002. Her experience began in title & escrow in Mt. Airy Maryland.

Upon starting her career as a realtor in 2005 the Real Estate market took a turn for the worst. Claire listed & sold an abundance of distressed homes including, foreclosure & short sales. After experiencing the crash of the real estate market while witnessing another market shift all with a large decrease in inventory, she was presented with an opportunity to source properties to several hedge funds, which then created a niche of becoming an“Off Market Specialist”. Today this is mostly commonly known as an agent selling pocket listings. With strategic marketing & years of experience in the local real estate market, Claire is able to assist sellers in selling their homes as “pocket listings”, “coming soon” & providing a “first look” opportunity for her buyers. This alone provides a competitive edge for all of her clients alike.
